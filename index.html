Contemporary Furniture for an Urban Flat

Choosing furniture can be hard as places like IKEA offer a wide array of styles, rooms and prices. As this is hard to navigate and choose from, this page shall serve as a collection of furniture to put in a lovely and modern new flat in the heart of Berlin.

The hub of excitement

The living room presents the place where all of your friends, family and yourself will come together to eat, drink, talk, play and enjoy yourselves. It is not only an oasis of comfort and soothing furniture but also invites anyone else to relax and take it the enjoyable atmosphere.

The sofabed will allow visitors to stay over and enjoy extra time with you, while also allowing you to have a stylish sofa when watching TV or reading. The industrial style lamp and specially framed side table also further complement the decor and add a certain flair to the room to make it more cosy. The relaxation chair allows you to rock back and forward while endulging yourself in a good book or surfing your tablet.

This furniture offers an amazing set of pieces that will complement any room and look lovely and inviting to any of its residents or visitors.

A soothing retreat

The bedroom allows you to retreat and find some peace and quiet among the outside buzz and lively neighbourhood. The bed bursts with plenty of storage to allow you to not only sleep in comfort but also not to worry about the tidbits lying around the room. The chair allows clothes that can't be stored in the spacious wardrobe to be put on there while deciding between outfits. The wardrobe also has plenty of storage to store his and her clothes safely and in an orderly fashion. The chest of drawers offers further storage for any item of clothing.
